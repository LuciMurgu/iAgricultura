# Farmer Pilot Demo Path

## Purpose

Prepare AgroUnu for real farmer pilot conversations. A farmer should understand within 30 seconds:
1. What AgroUnu is showing
2. Why it matters
3. What data is missing
4. What safe step comes next
5. What AgroUnu is NOT deciding automatically

## Recommended Demo Path (~15 minutes)

### A. Home — `/dashboard` (2 min)

**Show**: Deciziile săptămânii, context health, outcome cards, setup card

**Say**: "AgroUnu nu începe cu hărți sau dashboard-uri. Începe cu ce merită verificat."

**Don't say**: "AgroUnu recomandă...", "Cel mai bun...", "Trebuie să..."

### B. Setup — `/setup` (3 min)

**Show**: Missing data, minimum useful context, setup steps, demo form

**Say**: "Dacă lipsesc date, AgroUnu nu le ascunde. Le transformă în pași clari."

**Ask**: "Ce date aveți deja? Ce vă lipsește cel mai mult?"

### C. Ask AgroUnu — `/ask` (3 min)

**Show**: Guided questions, answer previews, human review markers

**Say**: "Nu este chatbot liber. Întrebările sunt ghidate de dovezi și limite de siguranță."

**Don't say**: "Întreabă orice", "AI-ul decide"

### D. Invoices / Buy Better — `/invoices` (2 min)

**Show**: Invoice analysis, alerts, stock

**Ask**: "Unde pierdeți mai mult: la cumpărare sau la vânzare?"

### E. Parcels / Fields — `/parcels` (2 min)

**Show**: Parcel map, crop structure

**Ask**: "Câte parcele lucrați? Ce culturi?"

### F. Cooperative — `/cooperative` (2 min)

**Show**: Cooperative network, non-binding signals

**Ask**: "Sunteți membru într-o cooperativă? V-ar ajuta?"

### G. More — `/more` (1 min)

**Show**: All technical routes, future routes

**Say**: "Toate modulele tehnice rămân accesibile. Navigarea e organizată pe ce contezi."

## Success Signals

- Farmer nods at "ce lipsește" (missing data)
- Farmer asks "pot adăuga datele mele?"
- Farmer says "asta chiar m-ar ajuta la finanțare"
- Farmer understands setup wizard flow
- Farmer trusts source labels

## Confusion Signals

- Farmer asks "dar ce face AI-ul?"
- Farmer expects chatbot
- Farmer looks for buy/sell buttons
- Farmer confused by technical routes
- Farmer overwhelmed by too many cards

## Safety Boundaries

- Never claim AgroUnu recommends specific actions
- Never claim eligibility, compliance, diagnosis
- Never claim prices are guaranteed
- Always show what data is missing
- Always show "ce nu face AgroUnu automat"
